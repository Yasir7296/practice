Terms & Conditions

From the Candidate:

1. I undertake that I am applying tor admission with the consent of my parents/guardian.

2. 1 also undertake that I was not admitted to any other college/university in Intermediate/BS Post-graduate class in the previous year. If proved otherwise at any stage, the college authorities reserve the right to cancel my admission to this college and forfeit all my dues. I further undertake that I have read all the rules and regulations in the prospectus relating to examination and admission to this college and shall abide by them.

3. I also undertake that: (i) I have never been rusticated nor expelled from any institution, (ii) I have never taken part in any illegal activity in any institution nor has any disciplinary action ever been taken against me.

4. I undertake that I am not employed full or part time anywhere at the time of my classes in this college.

5. I undertake that I will not join any political organization during my stay in this college. If I do so. the college authorities reserve the right to cancel my admission.


From the Parents/Guardian:

6, I. the parents/guardian, undertake that I shall be responsible for the payment of college fee and all other dues of my son/daughter and shall indemnify for all the losses to the college due to his/her lapses.

7, I. the parents/guardian, also undertake that my son/daughter shall not take part in any political activity. Moreover, if. at any stage, the documents submitted by him/her are proved fake, forged or bogus, the college authorities reserve the right to cancel his/her admission and take necessary action accordingly.
